About

I am an undergraduate student identifying strictly as a learner. My goal is not to present myself as an expert, but as a disciplined student of computer systems.

I focus on building tools from scratch (like protocols and malware simulators) because I believe abstraction is the enemy of deep understanding. All work featured here is built for educational labs and research environments.

Snapper

Goal: To manually implement ARP spoofing and packet dissection to understand layer 2 attacks.
Current State: Python script utilizing raw sockets and Scapy library.
What I Learned: Network stability is fragile; improper ARP restoration immediately crashes the local segment.

Next Steps:

  • Implement proper async queue handling for packet traffic.
  • Add safeguards to prevent accidental DoS in lab environments.
  • Python (Scapy)
  • Raw Sockets
  • Network Protocols
